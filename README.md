# Creating a VPN Connection

1. Start up the lab environment. We will be using Windows OS.

2. Open Control Panel and look for "Network and Internet" > and then select "Network and Sharing Center".

3. Click on Change adapter settings.

4. Now we are going to create a VPN connection. To do this, click "Set up a new connection or network".

<img width="443" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e9ee80c5-baf8-4649-9cf2-80b75fff893c" />

Result of Step 4

5. Now click "Connect to a workplace".

<img width="437" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f98e24fd-8c8f-4eb9-a1d1-b86626c3d38d" />

Result of Step 5

6. Now click Use my Internet connection (VPN)

<img width="365" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/1e31c5fb-2f4d-4f38-bc60-ca24484fb523" />

Result of Step 6

7. Now fill the Internet address field with the public address of your network's VPN concentrator

<img width="353" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/03bc52c2-6ad5-4387-a847-8bce9f1b157d" />

Result of Step 7 and 8

8. Fill the Destination name field with a name for you to know what the connection is.

9. Now select Create.

10. To see if our VPN was created and can be selected. We are going to go to the PC settings.

11. Select "Network and Internet" and then click "VPN".

<img width="446" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a9e0a895-7fa2-435d-9b72-8c9fb572cd05" />

Result of Step 11

12. Click on the VPN you created and select "Advanced options".

<img width="245" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/9524604b-77b0-42f8-af9b-afd9acc00235" />

Result of Step 12

13. Click "Edit" and click VPN type. 

<img width="443" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/142f7bae-cb4c-43db-bb2f-2448befedf16" />

Result of Step 13

14. Click "Type of sign-in info".

<img width="243" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ffb0e086-0acf-489e-b914-cad53399c172" />

Result of Step 14

15. Now we are going to set up a Proxy Server for the VPN.

16. Scroll down your selected VPN where you can see "VPN proxy settings" and click "Manual setup".

<img width="215" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8833d8ce-dc1c-4d1d-92ef-b673d9d6ccbe" />

Result of Step 16

17. Now type in the public address of your network's VPN concentrator and set the port to 80.

<img width="203" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/66efdb66-6d15-4e92-8d61-2ac616b075a8" />

Result of Step 17

18. Now click the checkmark "Don't use the proxy server for local (intranet) addresses" and then click "Apply".

19. We officially created a VPN creation.
